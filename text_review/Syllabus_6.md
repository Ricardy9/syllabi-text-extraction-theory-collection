             This course is intended as an introduction to the newly emerging field of political econ-
          omy of institutions and development. Its purpose is to give you both a sense of the frontier
          research topics and a good command of the tools in the area. The reading list is inten-
          tionally long, to give those of you interested in the field an opportunity to dig deeper into
          some of the topics in this area. The lectures will cover the material with *’s in detail and
          also discuss the material without *’s, but in less detail.              
                                                                                  
             Grading: The course grade will be a combination of a final exam and approximately
          6 problem sets, with 25% weight on problem sets.
